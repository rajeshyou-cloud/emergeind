Weight contrast replaces colour to create hierarchy
The concept + the risk
  • Asymmetric split hero — the risk: blue column with "30+" as the only hero graphic. No image, no illustration. Confidence of the number doing all the work
  • Ultra-light weight display — 300w for headlines, with 800w bold for one word per line. Creates a rhythm that draws the eye
  • Electric blue as the only accent — used once in nav logo, once in hero strip, once in CTA. Every use feels deliberate
  • Most scalable for Phase 2 expansion; easier to maintain visual discipline
