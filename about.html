About Emerging Graphics

Decades of expertise. World-class technology. Pan-India service.

Our Story

A Legacy in Print & Packaging Technology

Emerging Graphics Pvt. Ltd. is a Navi Mumbai-based company specialising in solutions for the Indian printing and packaging industry. We are authorised distributors and service partners for globally reputed technology brands including EngView (Bulgaria), Purvar / Founder (China), IECHO (China) and KingT Digital Printers.

Founded with a mission to bring world-class print and packaging technology to the Indian market, we have grown to serve clients across the full spectrum of the industry — from commercial printers and packaging converters to pharmaceutical labels and industrial packaging manufacturers.

We provide the full spectrum of post-sale services including Annual Maintenance Contracts, equipment installation & commissioning, technical consulting, operator training, and spare parts supply — covering clients pan-India from our offices in Navi Mumbai, Bengaluru and New Delhi, and our head office in Dubai, UAE.
