KingT Digital Corrugation Printers

No plates. No pre-coating. Epson I3200-A printheads printing directly on corrugated board at industrial speed.

Overview

Digital Printing for Corrugated Packaging

KingT digital printers deliver CMYK full-colour inkjet output directly onto corrugated board — without printing plates, without pre-coating, and without the cost and lead time of conventional flexo. Established in 2009 and certified to European CE standards, KingT machines are trusted by corrugated box plants and display manufacturers worldwide.

Emerging Graphics is the authorised distributor and service partner for KingT in India, providing installation, commissioning, operator training and Annual Maintenance Contracts.

Key Features

What Makes KingT Different

⚡

One-Pass Technology

Corrugated board passes below the printhead only once — enabling mass production speeds without sacrificing quality.

🚫

No Pre-Coating Required

Print directly on corrugated board, kraft paper, honeycomb panel and coated paper — no surface prep needed.

🖨

Epson I3200-A Printhead

600 dpi / 1200 dpi optional. Flexible ink volume with physical precision — industrial-grade reliability.

💧

Water-based CMYK Inks

Fully enclosed automatic ink refilling system — no wastewater, no plate washing, environmentally friendly.

🤖

Intelligent Control

Full computer operation with leading-edge automatic feeder. No specialized printing technicians required.

🔄

Fast Job Switching

Plateless design enables "one-piece order printed immediately" — from file to finished box in minutes.

📐

Y-Axis Auto-Correction

High-precision servo motor corrects beam deviation automatically for consistent registration across every sheet.

📦

Space-Saving Footprint

Occupies approximately 25% of the floor space of a conventional flexo press — ideal for existing plant layouts.
