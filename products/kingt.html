KingT Digital Corrugation Printers

High-speed, high-quality digital inkjet printing directly on corrugated board — single-pass technology for the modern corrugated box plant.

Overview

Digital Printing for Corrugated Packaging

KingT digital printers deliver image-grade inkjet output directly onto corrugated board — without printing plates, without pre-coating, and without the cost and lead time of conventional flexo. Designed for corrugated box plants and display manufacturers, KingT machines enable short-run, personalised and on-demand print production with wide colour gamut and multi-grayscale quality.

Emerging Graphics is the authorised partner for KingT in India, providing installation, commissioning, training and after-sales service.
