IECHO Digital Cutting Systems

From compact sample makers to large-format multi-industry cutters — 9 models covering flatbed digital cutting, automated production lines, and rotary die cutting for labels and tags.

Overview

About IECHO

IECHO has specialised in digital cutting technologies for over 20 years, serving packaging, signage, advertising, automotive, apparel, furniture and composite industries worldwide. Their flatbed digital cutting tables use servo-driven cutting heads with interchangeable tools — oscillating blades, routers, creasing wheels and kiss-cut tools — for high-speed, high-precision output across a wide range of materials. The IECHO range also includes the MCT magnetic rotary die cutter for high-speed label and tag production.

Emerging Graphics is IECHO's authorised distributor and service partner for India, providing the full range of 9 models alongside installation, commissioning, operator training and Annual Maintenance Contracts.
