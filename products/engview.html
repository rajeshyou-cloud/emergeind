EngView — Packaging Design Suite

CAD/CAM software for structural packaging design — from concept to production-ready output, with 3D visualisation, AR export, and direct CNC machine integration.

Overview

What is EngView?

EngView Package & Display Designer Suite is a comprehensive CAD/CAM platform for structural packaging design. Developed in Bulgaria, it provides a complete workflow from creative concept to production-ready output — covering 3D visualisation, cutting die design, and direct CNC machine output for structural designers, graphic designers, diemakers, and sales teams.

EngView includes a library of hundreds of parametric structural designs that can be resized instantly by changing dimensions — dramatically accelerating the development cycle for folding cartons, corrugated packaging, retail displays and point-of-sale structures. It integrates with Adobe Illustrator, CorelDRAW, and leading CNC cutting machinery.

Key Features

What EngView Does Best

📐

Parametric Design Library

Hundreds of parametric structural designs — resize any box or display instantly by changing dimensions.

🔲

Billboard-quality 3D Visualisation

Real-time 3D folding simulation with graphic artwork draped onto structures for photorealistic client presentations.

🥽

AR Export (GLB / glTF)

Export 3D packaging models as GLB or glTF files for augmented reality product visualisation.

🌐

Browser-based 3D Sharing

Share interactive 3D packaging designs with customers via a web browser — no software required on their end.

🔗

CNC Machine Output

Direct output to IECHO, Kongsberg and other cutting table formats — no manual programming.

🎨

Adobe Illustrator & CorelDRAW Integration

Import artwork from Illustrator and CorelDRAW and apply it directly to the 3D packaging model.

✏️

Two-click Panel Folding

Intuitive face-to-face panel folding in two clicks — significantly faster than manual dieline construction.

🔄

Workflow Extension to 3D Rendering

Push packaging designs directly to 3D rendering software for packaging mock-ups and product photography.

📊

Bleed Conflict Resolution

Automatic detection and resolution of bleed conflicts in layouts — catches production errors before the cutter.

📁

Standard Format Export

Export in DXF, PDF and other standard pre-press and manufacturing formats.
