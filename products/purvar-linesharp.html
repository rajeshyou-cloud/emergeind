Overview

What is Purvar LineSharp?

Purvar LineSharp Anti-Counterfeiting Design System is a specialised security design software developed for creating advanced anti-counterfeit features used in secure documents, packaging, labels, certificates, tax stamps, and brand protection applications.

The system enables designers and security printers to generate highly complex graphic elements that are extremely difficult to reproduce using conventional scanning, copying, or digital duplication methods. LineSharp combines precision vector graphics, Guilloche design, micro-pattern generation, engraving simulation, and other security technologies to help organisations protect products, documents, and brands from counterfeiting and forgery.

Widely used in government security printing, pharmaceutical packaging, luxury goods authentication, educational certificates, and high-value commercial products. Emerging Graphics is the authorised distributor for Purvar LineSharp in India and the Middle East.
