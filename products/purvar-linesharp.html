Purvar LineSharp — Line Art Enhancement

AI-assisted line art and text sharpening technology for consistently superior print output across all substrates.

Note: Detailed product specifications for Purvar LineSharp are being confirmed with our principal. Contact us directly for the current feature sheet and pricing.
Overview

What is Purvar LineSharp?

Purvar LineSharp is a pre-press enhancement tool that sharpens and optimises fine line art, text and vector elements before platemaking — maintaining crisp reproduction of fine details on high-speed offset and flexographic presses.

A new addition to the Emerging Graphics portfolio, LineSharp works alongside Purvar PaSharp or as a standalone enhancement within existing pre-press workflows. Contact us for a demonstration with your own artwork files.

Key Features

LineSharp Capabilities

🔍

AI Line Sharpening

Intelligent edge detection and sharpening preserves fine detail in logos, text and barcodes.

✍️

Fine Text Optimisation

Automatic thickening of hairlines and thin strokes to meet minimum printable widths.

📊

Barcode Enhancement

Bar width reduction and optical gain compensation for scannable barcodes every time.

🎯

Trapping Automation

Automated trap generation for all object types with user-configurable tolerance settings.

🖥

Before/After Preview

Real-time side-by-side comparison of original and enhanced artwork at full resolution.

⚡

Batch Processing

Process entire job folders automatically via hot folder integration.

🔗

PaSharp Integration

Seamless pipeline integration with Purvar PaSharp as a pre-processing stage.

📐

Resolution Independence

Works at any input resolution from 1200 to 4800 dpi output.

🎨

Spot Colour Aware

Processes each colour separation independently including Pantone and metallics.

📁

Non-destructive Workflow

Original files are never modified — all enhancements output to separate files.
