Purvar PaSharp — Pre-press Suite

Comprehensive pre-press workflow software for precision plate making, colour management and imposition.

Note: Detailed product specifications for Purvar PaSharp are being confirmed with our principal. Contact us directly for the current feature sheet and pricing.
Overview

What is Purvar PaSharp?

Purvar PaSharp is a pre-press software platform developed by Founder (China) for the printing and packaging industry. It covers colour separation, plate curve compensation, imposition, proofing and RIP output — compatible with leading CTP and conventional platesetters from Kodak, Agfa, Heidelberg and Screen.

Emerging Graphics is the authorised distributor for Purvar software in India and the Middle East. Contact us for a product demonstration and compatibility check with your existing pre-press environment.

Key Features

PaSharp Feature Set

🎨

Colour Separation

CMYK and spot colour separation with ICC profile support and GCR/UCR control.

📋

Imposition

Automatic imposition for sheetfed, web and digital presses with gang-run support.

📈

Dot Gain Compensation

Per-channel plate curve adjustment for consistent press results across substrates.

🖨

CTP / Platesetter Output

Direct output to Kodak Trendsetter, Agfa :Avalon, Heidelberg Suprasetter, Screen PT-R.

✅

Soft Proofing

On-screen soft proofing with calibrated monitor profiles for client sign-off.

🔄

Job Automation

Hot folder automation with job ticketing and batch processing for high-volume workflows.

📁

PDF/X Compliance

Preflight and normalisation to PDF/X-1a, PDF/X-3 and PDF/X-4 standards.

🔗

MIS Integration

JDF/JMF connectivity for integration with print MIS and ERP systems.

🛡

Preflight Engine

Comprehensive preflight checking with customisable rule sets and auto-fix capabilities.
