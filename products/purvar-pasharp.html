Overview

What is Purvar PaSharp?

Purvar PaSharp is a professional packaging prepress software developed by Purvar Technology (China) for converters, packaging manufacturers, label printers, and trade shops. Built on over 30 years of experience in graphic arts and packaging production, PaSharp delivers powerful tools for trapping, Step & Repeat layout creation, intelligent nesting, mark generation, and packaging artwork preparation.

Designed to streamline complex packaging workflows while reducing manual intervention and production errors, PaSharp supports both Adobe Illustrator workflows and high-speed batch processing. Recent versions include Variable Data Printing (VDP) capabilities for personalised packaging and labels.

Emerging Graphics is the authorised distributor for Purvar PaSharp in India and the Middle East.
